Cruise Travel Assistance & Insurance

Make sure you’ve got the right Travel Assistance and Insurance with our specialist Cruise Cover.

Why do you need a Specialist Cruise Cover?

Specialist cruise travel assistance and insurance offers cover for problems that could arise while you’re on a cruise - such as missed port departures or transport to a hospital if you fall ill while at sea.

  • All round protection - Provides cover not just on a cruise ship but for your entire trip.
  • Cruise cancellation or curtailment - Covers cancellation costs if you or a family member suffers from unforeseen medical circumstances.
  • Missed departure - If you can’t make your departure due you’re covered financially.
  • Cruise interruption - Protects travel expenses incurred to reach the next port in order to re-join the cruise.
  • Cabin confinement - Covers losses if you’re confined to your cabin by the ship’s medical officer due to illness.
  • Unused excursions protection - This benefit pays out if you miss an excursion because of an accident, injury or illness.
  • Missed port cover - Protects you if a planned destination visit is cancelled because of bad weather or timetabling.
  • Personal baggage - Covers baggage loss or its contents are lost or stolen.
  • Emergency medical treatment costs - Includes emergency helicopter transfers, hospital and ambulance fees, and cover to get home if you can’t use your original ticket. And much more…
